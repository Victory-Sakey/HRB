What We Do Here

Personal Care

Let our compassionate caregivers provide the helping hand your loved one needs to thrive at home. We offer a variety of personal care services to ensure their comfort, safety, and well-being, including:

  • • Activities of Daily Living (ADLs):  Bathing, dressing, toileting, transferring, ambulation (walking) assistance.
  • • Continence Care: Assisting with toileting needs and managing incontinence products.
  • • Mobility assistance: Helping clients move around safely within their home or navigate outings.
  • • Meal preparation: Planning, shopping for, and preparing nutritious meals according to dietary restrictions.
  • • Feeding assistanceHelping clients who have difficulty eating independently.
  • • Medication reminders and management (with limitations): Reminding clients to take medications and ensuring they are taken correctly.

Homemaking Services

Don’t let household chores become a burden. Our homemaking services free up your loved one’s time and energy, allowing them to focus on what matters most. We offer a helping hand with tasks like:

  • • Light housekeeping:   Dusting, vacuuming, laundry, dishes, and other light cleaning tasks.
  • • Grocery shopping and errands: Helping clients with shopping lists and running errands.
  • • Laundry and linen care: Washing, drying, folding, and ironing clothes and linens.

Companionship and Emotional Support

Our services extend beyond physical assistance. We understand the importance of social connection and emotional well-being. Our caregivers provide:

  • • Social interaction: Providing companionship to prevent isolation and loneliness.
  • • Engaging activities: Playing games, reading, listening to music, or participating in hobbies.
  • • Emotional support: Offering a listening ear and emotional support.
  • • Transportation assistance: Helping clients with transportation to appointments or outings.

Specialized Care

At Hope Rising Behavioral LLC , we recognize that some clients may require more specialized care. We offer a range of services to meet individual needs, including:

  • Skilled nursing care: Our licensed nurses are well experienced to attend to patients who need wound care, medication administration, and chronic disease management.
  • Respite care: Providing temporary relief for family caregivers.
  • Live-in care:  24/7 home home care services for clients who require constant supervision. (Private Live-In Caregiver)
  • Dementia care: Specialized care for clients with Alzheimer’s disease or other forms of dementia.
  • Personal care for specific needs: Care tailored to specific conditions like post-surgical recovery or rehabilitation.
  • Home Care for Stroke Patients
  • Home Care for Diabetes Patients
  • Home Care for the Disabled
  • Parkinson’s Home Care
